‘Conglomerate’

https://github.com/jmblake4/Conglomerate


• My final project from the developer bootcamp. Me and two other team members conceptualized the project, split the workload, used github for version control and gave a 15-minute presentation with a Q&A at the end.
• ‘Conglomerate’ is a ‘Social Media Manager’. It has a login page that gains access to the dashboard via parse.com (MongoDB). Once at the dashboard, you can access up to four different views at one time. They include ‘The Guardian’ news feed, a weather app that uses your geolocation, a YouTube app and a Movie Database app. Ultimately gmail, yahoo mail, twitter and reddit will be added as well.
• My role in the project was to ‘wire in’ all of the RESTful API’s, build the YouTube and movie app, design the logo and aid in the design of the site as well as help Jay with the authentication. I used 6 API’s total: Guardian, OpenWeatherMap, YouTube, OMDB, IMDB and parse.com.
• We built the site with Angular, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, Bootstrap, Onsen and LESS on the front-end/UI. We used node.js, ExpressJS and NoSQL on the back-end along with npm for packaging.
